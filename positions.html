Available Positions

Postdoc opportunity

There is a Postdoc opportunity in my research lab for an exciting robotics project in collaboration with an industrial partner. This position involves the development (as well as experimentation) of Advanced Control and Trajectory Optimization using Machine Learning techniques for an advanced mobile robot manipulator.  In addition to work with the industrial partner, a successful candidate is expected to publish the results of their work in high-quality journals.

Strong background in robotics and programming are required for this position. Candidates with strong background in control and optimization are encouraged to apply. It is also expected that applicants have a strong publication record.

Interested candidates who qualify for this position are invited to send a short description of how their expertise are relevant to the position, their detailed CV, as well as two of their best published journal papers (in the last two years) to me. After a careful review of all the applications, the suitable applicants will be formally interviewed.

The application deadline is May 1, 2021.

Important note for candidates: applicants for this position should have some background on robotics. If you haven’t taken any courses in robotics or don’t have any background in robotics please don’t contact to inquire about this position.

M.A.Sc / PhD opportunity

There is an opening in my research lab for M.A.Sc/PhD (fully funded) for an exciting project on mobile manipulator. This project will be in collaboration with an industrial partner. The applicant will be developing novel control algorithm for an advanced mobile manipulator. This project involves both control development and experiments. Candidates with strong background in control are encouraged to apply for this position. Some basic background in robotics will be very helpful but not necessary.

M.A.Sc. candidates: please send (i) a short description of how your skills are relevant to the position, (ii) your detailed CV, (iii) yor transcripts.

PhD candidates: please send (i) a short description of how your skills are relevant to the position, (ii) your detailed CV, (iii) your transcripts, (iv) one of your most important papers that you have published.

* Equity, diversity, and inclusion is strongly promoted in my research team. People from different disciplines regardless of their background, race, gender, etc. as well as aboriginal people, visible minorities, and females are all encouraged to apply. The CVs and academic records of all the candidates will be considered.
