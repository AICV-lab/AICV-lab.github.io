Available Positions

There are positions for one Postdoc and one M.A.Sc. in my research lab for exciting projects on mobile robot manipulators. These projects involve developing advanced control and optimization techniques using cutting edge methods including machine learning.

Postdoc candidate: Postdoc candidate should have strong background in advanced control, fundamentals of robotics, optimization and it would be a big plus if they have machine learning experience as well.

M.A.Sc. candidate: The applicant should have background in robotics. Only students who have officially taken any course(s) in robotics or/and have done at least a project in robotics should contact me to inquire about this position.

Important note for candidates: These projects require students to have some background (at least) on robotics. If you haven’t taken any courses in robotics or don’t have any background in robotics please don’t contact to inquire about these positions.
